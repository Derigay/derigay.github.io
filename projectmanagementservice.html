PROJECT MANAGEMENT
At Gay Custom Estates, we offer project management services to help you bring your dream home to life with ease. We will act as a coordinator, handling every detail of the construction process from start to finish. Unlike our custom home building service, which involves a more hands-on approach to the construction process and our own sub-contractors, our project management service is designed for homeowners who have a specific set of tradespeople they would like to work with but need assistance in coordinating the project and all of the nitty gritty details. We will work closely with you and your selected trades to ensure that every detail is handled seamlessly, from scheduling to budgeting. We believe in transparency and frequent communication throughout the construction process, keeping you informed every step of the way. With our commitment to building with intention, we'll ensure that your vision is brought to life with excellence and care, while you can relax knowing that every detail is being taken care of.

Project Management Process

1. Contractor Management

We will handle the coordination and scheduling of various contractors needed for the project, ensuring that they arrive on time and complete their work to the highest standards.


2. Budgeting and Cost Control

We will help you develop a budget for your project and keep track of expenses throughout the process. We will also ensure that all decisions made are cost effective, as well as use our industry experience to identify potential cost savings without sacrificing quality.

3. Decision-Making Support

With so many choices, you can rely on us to help you make important decisions throughout the construction process, from selecting building materials to choosing finishes. We ensure that you have all information required to help you make informed choices that you feel confident in.


4. Communication & Timeline Management

We act as your central point of contact, keeping you informed of progress and managing the overall timeline. We facilitate all communication between you, your selected tradespeople, and other stakeholders to ensure a seamless construction experience, that's on time and on budget.
