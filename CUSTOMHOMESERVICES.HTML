CUSTOM HOME BUILDING
At Gay Custom Estates, we're passionate about building homes that are as unique and special as our clients. Our process starts with a consultation where we work closely with you to discover your needs and preferences for your dream home. Together, we move on to the design phase, creating a customized plan that perfectly reflects your personal style and practical requirements. What sets us apart is our strong relationships with trusted subcontractors, ensuring superior quality and timely workmanship, and transparent communication with our clients throughout the entire process, day or night. Our generational home building experience means that we have the knowledge and expertise to make your vision a reality. And once your home is complete, we continue to provide support to ensure your complete satisfaction. At Gay Custom Estates, we believe in building more than just houses – we build homes and relationships that last a lifetime.

Custom Home Building Process

1. Consultation

During the consultation phase, we work closely with you to understand your needs, preferences, and budget, ensuring that your vision for your dream home is fully realized.

2. Design

During this phase we create a unique and personalized plan that perfectly reflects your style and practical requirements, truly bringing your dream home to life.

3. Finalization & Permits

During this phase, we sent off drawings to engineers and handle necessary permits to solidify your design.


4. Selections & Change Orders

We then closely with you to choose the highest-quality materials and finishes that meet your needs and style preferences. We also introduce you to our system that handles any necessary alterations during the process.

5. Construction Process

Next, our experienced team of builders work diligently to ensure that every aspect of the build meets your expectations, while providing regular updates and transparent communication at every stage.

6. Walkthrough & Continued Support

We then conduct a thorough final walkthrough with you to ensure that every detail is perfect and provide continued support, addressing any questions or concerns that may arise long after you move in.
