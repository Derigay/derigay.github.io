THE GAY CUSTOM ESTATES DIFFERENCE

5TH GENERATION IN THE DEVELOPMENT BUSINESS

We're not your average custom home builder. We're a 5th generation family in the development business, with a lifetime of hands-on experience in the development industry. From the time we small children, we've been on job sites, learning the ins and outs of home building from the ground up. This experience has instilled in us a deep passion for building homes that are not only functional and beautiful, but also stand the test of time.

EXTENSIVE, STRONG WEB OF INDUSTRY CONTACTS

We've spent generations building up a broad and strong web of industry contacts. When it comes to sourcing the best materials and subcontractorsfor your project, we don't just rely on chance - we call on the people we know and trust. This means we can ensure every aspect of your project is executed to perfection, on time, and on budget. We take great pride in our web of contacts and it's a testament to the relationships we've built over generations in the development industry.

TRANSPARENT COMMUNICATION & CLIENT-CENTRIC APPROACH

We know that building a home is a huge emotional and financial investment. That's why we make transparent and frequent communication a priority, so that you can remain informed and prepared from initial consultation to final walk-through. Our responsive and communicative team is always available to answer questions, address concerns, and provide regular updates on project status.

DERICK GAY


Derick Gay is a co-owner of Gay Custom Estates, with over 20 years of general construction experience. Having grown up in a family of developers, he has been immersed in the industry from a young age, and his expertise in custom home building is unmatched. Derick's organizational skills are essential to the success of each project, as he manages all subcontractors and processes with ease, ensuring that each stage of construction is executed efficiently and on schedule. He is well-versed in the complexities of building permits and regulations and excels at navigating these often-complicated requirements. Derick's attention to detail, process-oriented approach, and experience in plumbing and HVAC mean that clients can trust that their project is in good hands with him at the helm.

RICK GAY


Rick Gay is a co-owner of Gay Custom Estates, bringing extensive hands-on experience to the team. With his deep understanding of every construction phase of building a home, Rick is a skilled worker who is intimately familiar with all aspects of custom home building. He is known for his ability to provide valuable insights into each project, ensuring that everything is executed to the highest standards. Rick's hands-on experience also enables him to work alongside the team, assisting with some of the phases to ensure that everything is completed to perfection. As a co-owner, Rick is involved in all aspects of the business, from initial client meetings to the final walkthrough. His dedication to quality and his wealth of experience leads to strong client trust and a flawless finished product.
